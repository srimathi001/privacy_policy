Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Welcome to Destino (Weekend). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

When you visit our mobile application "Destino" and use our services, you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering at the App, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when planning trips, or otherwise contacting us.

  • Personal Identity: Name, Profile information.
  • Contact Data: Phone numbers (used for secure login and OTP verification) and Email addresses.
  • Location Data: We collect location data (Latitude/Longitude) to provide features like "Nearby Places," map navigation, and trip tracking.
  • Media & Storage: We may access your camera and gallery to allow you to upload profile photos or save trip memories, solely with your permission.
  • Trip Data: Information about your saved trips, itineraries, and preferences to provide personalized recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and secure logon process.
  • To allow you to plan, save, and manage your trips and itineraries.
  • To provide location-based services such as finding nearby tourist attractions.
  • To send administrative information to you, such as app updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To generate analysis graphs and statistics regarding your travel history.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may use third-party services (like Google Maps API for navigation) which may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us directly.
